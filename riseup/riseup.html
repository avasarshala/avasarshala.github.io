RiseUp! is a signature program by Avasarshala to Empower, Educate and Ensure girls from economically weaker sections of societies get equal access to Opportunities that help them Fulfil their Potential.





80% of the Growth Opportunities do not reach the right Women.

RiseUp! is designed to change this.




RiseUp! Signature Leadership Program for Girls



Level 01: Digital Assistance

Mobile platform provided by Avasarshala will help girls in the age group 6-18 years engage in opportunities that utilize their talents and interests.

Level 02: Personal Advisor Assistance

Every Girl receives one-to-one assistance regarding every Opportunity sent to her. The Personal Advisor acts as a Mentor to ensure appropriate opportunities are not missed out and help with the application processes if required.

Level 03: Awareness Camps for Moms

Moms who attend the camp will be provided with Awareness regarding the Opportunities, their benefits and way forward. They will earn the capacity to be the Girl's Mentors at Home

Level 04: RiseUp! Signature Leadership Program and Leadership Toolkit

High potential girls who demonstrate active participation and enthusiasm to achieve more are invited into the RiseUp! Signature Leadership Program. The cohort is guided to focus on leadership development objectives and start reflecting upon their personal and professional goals.

Impact and Outcome Expected from RiseUp!



10x Opportunity Access to Girls from Economically Weaker Sections
