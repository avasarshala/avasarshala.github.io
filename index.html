Co-Founder to Avasarshala, Ashwathy is a Kectil USA Board Member, a Youth Leadership Organization based in Atlanta, USA. An Ex-Amazonian and MBA Gold Medalist, Ashwathy has marked her presence in the Global Leadership landscape.
